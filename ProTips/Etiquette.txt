Quick aside about Etiquette, specificly the concept of keeping face

Samurai are never supposed to show emotion. Isawa smiling is about the societal equiv of running up and hugging someone. In a case like this is only minor and embarassing but there a few times that you should absolutely never let your emotions show.

-Around people of high status
-Around people of high honour
-Around people looking to start a fight (you dont become a famous duelist for being forgiving)

There are times when it is appropriate, it must always be a personal setting. Usually where you are the host and

-They are close family or friends
-You are trying to put a person at ease who is intimidated by your Status/Glory

Note: People below Status 1 are not expected to follow the code of the Bushido, they are the dregs of society.
