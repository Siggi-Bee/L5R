Heres how Void Points can be spent:

-Add 1k1 to any skill (Not Damage)
-Temprorarily know any skill (Treated as Rank 1)
-Soak 10 wounds of damage
-Increase you Armour TN by 10
-Trade initiative with a willing participant (both must spend Void)
-Add 10 to your current initiative (new score is kept for rest of the skirmish)

Some school techs requires that you spend void to use them.



How to recover Void Points:

-Rest for 8 hours to fully recover
-The Meditate Skill recovers 1
-A successful Tea Ceremony recovers 1 per participant
